The Internationalization Attributes

lang = "en"

Used to define the Language used in HTML

dir = "ltr"

Used to define the Direction of TEXT/Content in HTML i.e Left to Right
The dots will be at the (Right) side of the Statment..........

dir = "rtl"

Used to define the Direction of TEXT/Content in HTML i.e Right to Left
The dots will be at the (Left) side of the Statment..........
