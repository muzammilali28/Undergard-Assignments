Pakistan is an Islamic Republic and was formed in 1947. The country was founded by Jinnah and
the country was separated from the United India to form Pakistan. Pakistan has 5 provinces and
it has India, Afghanistan, Iran and China as its neighbors. The country is being run according to
the principles of Islam and the country is in its worst state right now. Pakistan is a victim of severe
corruption, power outage and mismanagement. Most people are leaving the country as they
cannot live this difficult life. The country has rich mineral resources and great manpower.
